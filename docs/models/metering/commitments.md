# Commitments

Spend commitments for this rate card. Only applicable to usage-based prices
(unit, graduated, volume).


## Fields

| Field                                                   | Type                                                    | Required                                                | Description                                             |
| ------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------- |
| `MinimumAmount`                                         | `*string`                                               | :heavy_minus_sign:                                      | The customer is committed to spend at least the amount. |
| `MaximumAmount`                                         | `*string`                                               | :heavy_minus_sign:                                      | The customer is limited to spend at most the amount.    |
